Abstract

摘要

This document describes the SEED encryption algorithm, which has been adopted by most of the security systems in the Republic of Korea. Included are a description of the cipher and the key scheduling algorithm (Section 2), the S-boxes (Appendix A), and a set of test vectors (Appendix B).

本文件描述了种子加密算法，该算法已被大韩民国大多数安全系统采用。包括密码和密钥调度算法（第2节）、S盒（附录a）和一组测试向量（附录B）的说明。

1. Introduction
1. 介绍
1.1. SEED Overview
1.1. 种子概述

SEED is a 128-bit symmetric key block cipher that has been developed by KISA (Korea Information Security Agency) and a group of experts since 1998. SEED is a national standard encryption algorithm in South Korea [TTASSEED] and is designed to use the S-boxes and permutations that balance with the current computing technology. It has the Feistel structure with 16-round and is strong against DC (Differential Cryptanalysis), LC (Linear Cryptanalysis), and related key attacks, balanced with security/efficiency trade-off.

SEED是一种128位对称密钥分组密码，由KISA（韩国信息安全局）和一组专家自1998年以来开发。SEED是韩国的一种国家标准加密算法[TTASSEED]，旨在使用与当前计算技术相平衡的S盒和排列。它具有16轮的Feistel结构，能够抵御DC（差分密码分析）、LC（线性密码分析）和相关的密钥攻击，并在安全性/效率权衡的基础上保持平衡。

The features of SEED are outlined as follows:

SEED的特点概述如下：

- The Feistel structure with 16-round - 128-bit input/output data block size - 128-bit key length - A round function strong against known attacks - Two 8x8 S-boxes - Mixed operations of XOR and modular addition

- 具有16轮的Feistel结构-128位输入/输出数据块大小-128位密钥长度-一个抗已知攻击能力强的轮函数-两个8x8 S盒-异或和模加的混合操作

SEED has been widely used in South Korea for confidential services such as electronic commerce; e.g., financial services provided in wired and wireless communication.

SEED在韩国广泛用于电子商务等保密服务；e、 例如，通过有线和无线通信提供的金融服务。

2. The Structure of SEED
2. 种子结构

The input/output block size of SEED is 128-bit, and the key length is also 128-bit. SEED has the 16-round Feistel structure. A 128-bit input is divided into two 64-bit blocks (L, R), and the right 64-bit block is an input to the round function F, with a 64-bit subkey Ki generated from the key schedule.

种子的输入/输出块大小为128位，密钥长度也为128位。种子具有16个圆形的Feistel结构。128位输入分为两个64位块（L，R），右64位块是舍入函数F的输入，其中64位子键Ki由键调度生成。

A pseudo code for the structure of SEED is as follows:

SEED结构的伪代码如下所示： 

     for (i = 1; i <= 16; i++)
     {
       L = R;
       R = L ^ F(Ki, R);
     }
        
     for (i = 1; i <= 16; i++)
     {
       L = R;
       R = L ^ F(Ki, R);
     }
2.1. The Round Function F
2.1. 圆函数F

SEED uses two 8x8 S-boxes, permutations, rotations, and basic modular operations such as exclusive OR (XOR) and additions to provide strong security, high speed, and simplicity in its implementation.

SEED使用两个8x8 S盒、置换、旋转和基本模块化操作，如异或（XOR）和加法，以提供强大的安全性、高速性和简单性。

A 64-bit input block of the round function F is divided into two 32-bit blocks (R0, R1) and wrapped with 4 phases:

圆形函数F的64位输入块分为两个32位块（R0、R1），并用4个相位包装：

- A mixing phase of two 32-bit subkey blocks (Ki0 , Ki1) - 3 layers of function G (See Section 2.2), with additions for mixing two 32-bit blocks

- 两个32位子键块（Ki0，Ki1）的混合阶段-3层函数G（见第2.2节），添加用于混合两个32位块的附加值

The outputs (R0', R1') of function F are as follows:

功能F的输出（R0'，R1'）如下所示： 

     R0' = G[ G[ G[(R0 ^ Ki0) ^ (R1 ^ Ki1)] + (R0 ^ Ki0)] + G[(R0 ^ Ki0)
            ^ (R1 ^ Ki1)]] + G[ G[(R0 ^ Ki0) ^ (R1 ^ Ki1)] + (R0 ^ Ki0)]
        
     R0' = G[ G[ G[(R0 ^ Ki0) ^ (R1 ^ Ki1)] + (R0 ^ Ki0)] + G[(R0 ^ Ki0)
            ^ (R1 ^ Ki1)]] + G[ G[(R0 ^ Ki0) ^ (R1 ^ Ki1)] + (R0 ^ Ki0)]
        
     R1' = G[ G[ G[(R0 ^ Ki0) ^ (R1 ^ Ki1)] + (R0 ^ Ki0)] + G[(R0 ^ Ki0)
            ^ (R1 ^ Ki1)]] + G[ G[(R0 ^ Ki0) ^ (R1 ^ Ki1)]
        
     R1' = G[ G[ G[(R0 ^ Ki0) ^ (R1 ^ Ki1)] + (R0 ^ Ki0)] + G[(R0 ^ Ki0)
            ^ (R1 ^ Ki1)]] + G[ G[(R0 ^ Ki0) ^ (R1 ^ Ki1)]
2.2. The Function G
2.2. 函数G

The function G has two layers: a layer of two 8x8 S-boxes and a layer of block permutation of sixteen 8-bit sub-blocks. The outputs Z (= Z0 || Z1 || Z2 || Z3) of the function G with four 8-bit inputs X (= X0 || X1 || X2 || X3) are as follows:

函数G有两层：一层是两个8x8 S盒，一层是16个8位子块的块置换。具有四个8位输入X（=X0 | X1 | X2 | X3）的函数G的输出Z（=Z0 | Z1 | Z2 | Z3）如下所示： 

    Z0 = {S1(X0) & m0} ^ {S2(X1) & m1} ^ {S1(X2) & m2} ^ {S2(X3) & m3}
    Z1 = {S1(X0) & m1} ^ {S2(X1) & m2} ^ {S1(X2) & m3} ^ {S2(X3) & m0}
    Z2 = {S1(X0) & m2} ^ {S2(X1) & m3} ^ {S1(X2) & m0} ^ {S2(X3) & m1}
    Z3 = {S1(X0) & m3} ^ {S2(X1) & m0} ^ {S1(X2) & m1} ^ {S2(X3) & m2}
        
    Z0 = {S1(X0) & m0} ^ {S2(X1) & m1} ^ {S1(X2) & m2} ^ {S2(X3) & m3}
    Z1 = {S1(X0) & m1} ^ {S2(X1) & m2} ^ {S1(X2) & m3} ^ {S2(X3) & m0}
    Z2 = {S1(X0) & m2} ^ {S2(X1) & m3} ^ {S1(X2) & m0} ^ {S2(X3) & m1}
    Z3 = {S1(X0) & m3} ^ {S2(X1) & m0} ^ {S1(X2) & m1} ^ {S2(X3) & m2}

where m0 = 0xfc, m1 = 0xf3, m2 = 0xcf, and m3 = 0x3f.

其中，m0=0xfc，m1=0xf3，m2=0xcf，m3=0x3f。

To increase the efficiency of G function, four extended S-boxes 'SS-box' (See Appendix A.2) are defined as follows:

为提高G功能的效率，四个扩展S盒“SS盒”（见附录A.2）定义如下： 

    SS0(X)= {S1(X) & m3} || {S1(X) & m2} || {S1(X) & m1} || {S1(X) & m0}
    SS1(X)= {S2(X) & m0} || {S2(X) & m3} || {S2(X) & m2} || {S2(X) & m1}
    SS2(X)= {S1(X) & m1} || {S1(X) & m0} || {S1(X) & m3} || {S1(X) & m2}
    SS3(X)= {S2(X) & m2} || {S2(X) & m1} || {S2(X) & m0} || {S2(X) & m3}
        
    SS0(X)= {S1(X) & m3} || {S1(X) & m2} || {S1(X) & m1} || {S1(X) & m0}
    SS1(X)= {S2(X) & m0} || {S2(X) & m3} || {S2(X) & m2} || {S2(X) & m1}
    SS2(X)= {S1(X) & m1} || {S1(X) & m0} || {S1(X) & m3} || {S1(X) & m2}
    SS3(X)= {S2(X) & m2} || {S2(X) & m1} || {S2(X) & m0} || {S2(X) & m3}

New G function, Z, can be defined as follows:

新的G函数Z可定义如下： 

      Z = SS0(X0) ^ SS1(X1) ^ SS2(X2) ^ SS3(X3)
        
      Z = SS0(X0) ^ SS1(X1) ^ SS2(X2) ^ SS3(X3)

This new G function is faster than the original G function but takes more memory to store four SS-boxes.

这个新的G函数比原来的G函数快，但是需要更多的内存来存储四个SS盒。

2.3. Key Schedule
2.3. 关键时刻表

The key schedule generates each round subkeys. It uses the function G, addition in modular 2**32, subtraction in modular 2**32, and (left/right) circular rotation. A 128-bit input key is divided into four 32-bit blocks (Key0, Key1, Key2, Key3). The two 32-bit subkeys of the ith round, Ki0 and Ki1, are generated as follows:

关键点明细表生成每轮子关键点。它使用函数G、模2**32中的加法、模2**32中的减法以及（左/右）循环旋转。128位输入键分为四个32位块（键0、键1、键2、键3）。第i轮的两个32位子键Ki0和Ki1生成如下：

- Type 1 : Odd round Ki0 = G(Key0 + Key2 - KCi) Ki1 = G(Key1 - Key3 + KCi) Key0 || Key1 = (Key0 || Key1) >> 8

- 类型1：奇数轮Ki0=G（Key0+Key2-KCi）Ki1=G（Key1-Key3+KCi）Key0 | | Key1=（Key0 | | Key1）>>8

- Type 2 : Even round Ki0 = G(Key0 + Key2 - KCi) Ki1 = G(Key1 - Key3 + KCi) Key2 || Key3 = (Key2 || Key3) << 8

- 类型2：偶数圆形Ki0=G（键0+Key2-KCi）Ki1=G（键1-Key3+KCi）键2 | |键3=（键2 | |键3）<<8

2.4. Decryption Procedure
2.4. 解密程序

Decryption procedure is the reverse step of the encryption procedure. It can be implemented by using the encryption algorithm with reverse order of the round subkeys.

解密过程与加密过程相反。它可以通过使用与圆形子密钥顺序相反的加密算法来实现。
